# International Shipping Optimizer
跨境物流策略与承运商评估框架，覆盖速度、成本、清关和追踪的全维度对比。

## What it does
- 🚢 承运商多维评分：DHL、FedEx、UPS、海运等 8 家承运商在速度、成本、可靠性上的量化对比
- 📍 线路推荐：针对中国到美国、欧洲、日本等主要目的地的定制运输方案
- 💰 成本优化策略：拼柜、集货、第三方物流平台报价对比，提供 5+ 省钱方案

## Example scenarios

**1. 义乌小商品发美国运费优化**
> 👤 我在义乌做小商品出口，每次发美国海运一个小柜要五六千，听说有更便宜的拼柜渠道，帮我算算怎么优化？
> 🤖 对比整柜(FCL)与拼箱(LCL)的运费差异，推荐拼箱到洛杉矶/长滩港。评估义乌到宁波港短驳费用优化，对比菜鸟国际、4PX等第三方平台报价，综合VAT/duty和清关费用。

**2. Amazon FBA 卖家选物流**
> 👤 I'm an Amazon FBA seller. I need to ship 200kg of electronics from Shenzhen to Amazon US warehouses. What's the best option?
> 🤖 Compare DHL, FedEx, sea freight, SF Express across speed, cost/kg, customs handling, and Amazon delivery requirements. Recommend split strategy (urgent via air, bulk via sea) with cost comparison table and estimated transit times.

**3. 大宗货物运输方式对比**
> 👤 Shipping 500kg of furniture from Guangzhou to Netherlands — should I use LCL sea freight or air freight?
> 🤖 Break down cost, transit time, customs documentation, VAT/duty implications, and insurance for both options. Recommend LCL sea freight with buffer stock strategy and side-by-side comparison table.
